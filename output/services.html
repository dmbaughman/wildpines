Services

Counseling

I specialize in adolescent emotional and behavioral counseling. I have an eclectic theoretical orientation based on solution focused, person centered, and cognitive behavioral therapies. My background in experiential education and adventure based therapy also influences my counseling.

Custody Supervised Visitation

During custody disputes, sometimes the court orders a third party to supervise visit between a non custodial parent and their children. I offer supervised visitation services on as needed basis.

Custody Case Management

I provide custody case management services to help facilitate communication and the implementation of parenting plans.
